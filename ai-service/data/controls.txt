Password policies must enforce minimum length and complexity requirements.
Accounts should be locked after multiple failed login attempts.
Sensitive data must be encrypted both at rest and in transit.
Access to critical systems must be logged and monitored.
Regular audits should be conducted to identify vulnerabilities.

Multi-factor authentication should be enabled for all privileged accounts.
Session timeouts must be enforced after inactivity.
Security patches must be applied regularly.
User access must follow least privilege principle.
Logs must be retained for audit purposes.
